### What I Have Built

<details>
<summary>Edge-Native Industrial SLM Diagnostic Assistant</summary>
<br>

An offline AI diagnostic assistant that runs entirely on a **Raspberry Pi 4** — no internet, no cloud, no external dependencies. Built for air-gapped industrial environments where connectivity is not an option.

Built with Phi-3 Mini, `llama.cpp`, ChromaDB, and a full RAG pipeline. Achieved a retrieval MRR of 0.83 and a time-to-first-token of 2.4 seconds under constrained hardware memory.

`Python` `llama.cpp` `ChromaDB` `LangChain` `Raspberry Pi 4`

</details>

<details>
<summary>NyayaGyan — Legal Intelligence Platform</summary>
<br>

A role-based AI legal assistant for document drafting, case-law retrieval, and intelligent search. Uses a hybrid retrieval approach combining BM25 and dense vector embeddings over a Neo4j legal knowledge graph.

Multi-user architecture backed by PostgreSQL pgvector for semantic similarity at scale.

`LangChain` `LangGraph` `Neo4j` `PostgreSQL` `FastAPI`

</details>

<details>
<summary>GMNE — Generative Multi-Physics Neural Emulator</summary>
<br>

Physics-Informed Neural Networks and DDPM models trained to replace slow CFD/FEM hydrogen electrolyzer simulations. The emulator achieves R² > 0.94 against ground-truth simulation data with sub-100ms inference latency.

Designed for trustworthy predictions — includes uncertainty quantification as a first-class output.

`PyTorch` `PINNs` `DDPM` `NumPy` `SciPy`

</details>

<details>
<summary>Autonomous KYB Agent — Corporate Due Diligence</summary>
<br>

Agentic AI system for automating Know-Your-Business due diligence workflows in corporate finance. Multi-agent orchestration handles document extraction, risk flagging, and structured report generation.

`LangGraph` `LLMs` `FastAPI` `Python`

</details>
